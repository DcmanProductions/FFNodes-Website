FFNodes Privacy Policy

1. No Information Collection

FFNodes does not collect, store, or process any personal or user information. We respect your privacy, and you can use the software without providing any personal data.

2. Usage Data

While using FFNodes, some non-personal usage data, such as IP addresses and device information, may be logged for operational purposes only. However, this data is not linked to any specific user and is used solely to ensure the functionality and security of the software. This information is only stored locally on your system.

3. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any non-personal usage data that may be collected, ensuring it is kept secure and confidential.

4. Changes to this Privacy Policy

If there are any changes to this privacy policy, they will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
